Emily O'Grady

Hi, I'm Emily O'Grady, a marine data scientist passionate about bridging technology and ocean science. Currently pursuing my Masters of Digital Innovation at Dalhousie University, I combine expertise in oceanographic data management with emerging technologies like AI to advance marine research. At the Bedford Institute of Oceanography, I lead data initiatives, develop R packages used nationally, and champion open science practices.
