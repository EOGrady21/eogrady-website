About

I'm a marine data scientist pursuing my Masters of Digital Innovation at Dalhousie University, where I'm exploring the intersection of AI and oceanographic research. At the Bedford Institute of Oceanography, I lead initiatives in data quality control, R package development, and collaborative research that advances our understanding of marine ecosystems through technology.

From developing the marea R package to chairing national support groups, I'm passionate about making ocean science more accessible and reproducible through innovative digital solutions.

Project Spotlight

QSEA: Agentic AI for Ocean Data

Developing intelligent agents for automated ocean data quality control, combining AI with expert domain knowledge to revolutionize oceanographic data management and ensure scientific integrity.

marea: Ecosystem Data Integration

Leading development of an R package that bridges oceanographic monitoring and fisheries ecosystem approaches, creating S4 classes and tools that make complex marine datasets accessible to researchers nationwide.

National Ocean Monitoring Programs

Managing quality control and archival of long-term ocean monitoring data (AZMP, AZOMP, BBMP), ensuring data meets international standards and remains accessible for climate research and ecosystem management.
